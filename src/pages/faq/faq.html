FAQ How Can I Subscribe to Clubs? Navigate to the "Clubs" tab, and then search for and select the club that interests you. From there, you can click "Subscribe" at the top! Why Can't I Subscribe? You can only subscribe to clubs and personalize your view if you're logged in. Be sure to log in with your UCF ID and Password so that you're not in guest mode. Where Can \n\nI See My Events? Select the Calendar tab where you can view the events you subscribed to and general events being held at UCF. How Do I Log Out?
